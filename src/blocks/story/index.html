Once upon a time...

When you're a viewer, you don't even think about how complicated the live broadcast is. But when you're a streamer, you are trying to find a way to stop pain. Our story begins on the day when engineer and co-founder Anton Kosykh decided to start doing his own live broadcast. Trying to make his own life better and easier, Anton could not even imagine that after some time, his tool will grow into a real product. That all happened when designer and co-founder Phil Murphy proposed to make a promo-site for his "tiny" project, in response to which Anton offered to build a startup...
